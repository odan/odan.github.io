Hello, my name is Daniel Opitz and I am a senior software developer and architect.

I create individual web applications for the public transport and e-government in Switzerland.

In my free time I contribute to various open source projects 
and like to **[blog](https://odan.github.io/)** about PHP, Slim-Framework and .NET.

You can find all my open source projects on [Github](https://github.com/odan).

Whenever possible I try to answer questions about Slim in the **[Slim-Forum](https://discourse.slimframework.com/)**
and on [StackOverflow](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/slim).

If you have questions about this eBook, please create an issue here: 
<https://github.com/odan/slim4-tutorial/issues>

You can **[contact](https://odan.github.io/about.html#contact)** me when you need 
private **consulting**, or you are in the process of migrating a particularly large 
project to a new version of PHP or Slim Framework.
